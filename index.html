Instructions

Objective: Type as many words correctly as you can within 99 seconds.

Starting the Game: Click on the input box labeled "click to start!" to begin the game. A new word will appear for you to type as soon as you correctly type the current word.

Scoring: You earn 1 point for each correct word typed.

Ending the Game:: The game ends when the timer reaches zero or when all words have been typed. The game can also be ended at any time by clicking the "Quit" button .

Restarting the Game: Click the "Restart" button to reset the timer, score, and words.

Scores: Click on the "Scores" button located at the top left corner of the screen to view the top 9 scores.
